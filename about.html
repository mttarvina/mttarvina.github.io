About this website

"Tarvs' Hobbytronics" is a collection of my project ideas in electronics. The website name was based on my nickname and was conceived way back 2019 during my first few months in my first job in the industry. At that time, I realized that I finally had the means to pay for a domain and explore publishing a personal website. I built this website mainly to document my projects, and to force myself to keep track of the things that I've been working on. I figured that it would be good to share these ideas with others. I'll be using this platform to document the things that I learn, and to share it to other aspiring electronic engineers. The projects that I'll be showcasing here will mostly contain at least one of the concepts in Power Electronics, Embedded Systems, Audio Electronics, Instrumentation, and Motor Control. These projects are mostly in hardware design, assembly, and fabrication; with some projects featuring embedded software/firmware written in programming languages like C, C++, and Python.

For inquiries, corrections, or if you want help on your own project, please refer to my Contact page.
